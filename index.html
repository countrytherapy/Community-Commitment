Our Community Commitment

Bringing high-quality, mobile occupational therapy to regional Victoria empowering children, families, and communities right where they are.

Transforming Lives in Regional Victoria

We travel to homes, schools, and facilities in towns like Stawell, Ararat, Horsham, Nhill, and beyond — removing barriers to care and helping people live more independently and meaningfully.

Our Community Programs

Pro Bono Work

Free or subsidised therapy for those facing financial or access barriers in regional areas.

  • Free assessments for low-income families
  • Volunteer support in aged care facilities
  • No-cost independence workshops
  • Assistance for DFFH tenancy clients

Helped 100+ individuals access essential care.

EmpowerEd Program

OT integrated into schools to support learning and development.

  • On-site support for motor skills & focus
  • Teacher training on inclusive strategies
  • Tailored plans in 40+ schools
  • Events for school readiness

Empowered hundreds of students to thrive.
